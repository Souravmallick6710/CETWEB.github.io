Achievements

"Optimism is the faith that leads to achievement. Nothing can be done without hope and confidence."
So here are some of the achievements by our students.

Amitash Nanda

Amitash Nanda of Instrumentation and Electronics Engineering (Passout Batch of 2019) has been accepted by the reputed universities in USA for Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering. According to THE ranking UCSD is ranked 33th position in the entire world. It is one of the top public universities in USA with best research opportunities. We congratulate Amitash on achieving this and we wish him all the very best for his future endeavours.

Anustup Biswal

Anustup Biswal of Instrumentation and Electronics Engineering of 2017 passout batch has been accepted by the prestigious McGill University for masters in Business Administration for fall 2021 . Anustup is the first CET Alumni to get into McGill MBA.McGill University is ranked among the top MBA schools in Canada that has students exchange program with IIM A and B. We congratulate Anustup on achieving this and we wish him all the very best for his future endeavours.

Muskan Khedia

Amid the pandemic,Muskan Khedia student of computer science and engineering,who got Microsoft job offer at Rs 43 lakh per annum. The Rs 43 lakh per annum package bagged by Muskan Khedia is the best salary package and is more than what the 2019-20 batch of the premier school had got. The best pay bundle which was given to the student last year was Rs 17.42 lakh annually.We congratulate Muskan on achieving this and we wish him all the very best for his future endeavours.

Pratyush Mallik

Odisha's Pratyush Mallick is creating ripples in the world of science. The final year Instrumentation & Electronic Engineering student from #CET is amongst the top-25 semi-finalists of @NASA's i-Tech initiative for innovations. His idea is a wireless system that uses standalone boxes to harvest energy from microbial fuel cells and will help monitor forest fires quickly. Probably detect a forest fire in Amazon before it hits the news? We are proud of you, Pratyush. Let's go make the country proud!
